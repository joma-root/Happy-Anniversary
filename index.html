I'm so grateful for you baby because you make life worth living. I know I am not the best partner in the world, but I will always try my best to be the best for you. Even if the cost of chasing our dreams is a long distance relationship, then it's ok kay wala mang choice HAHAHHHA, and ipagkakatiwala ko din sa ama.

Because no matter how many months and years we spend apart, every moment brings us closer to the future that we are building for. I promise po to always be there for you. To support your dreams, To be your safe space when the world gets too heavy. I promise to keep choosing you every day, just like you choose me. I love you so so much nica ko more than words that i can express.
